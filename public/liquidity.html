How This Data Is Fetched

GET /api/v3/exchangeInfo Lists all available trading pairs on Binance
  • We filter for stablecoin and fiat pairs only
  • Checks which pairs are actively trading
GET /api/v3/ticker/24hr?symbol=XXXYYY 24-hour rolling statistics for a trading pair
  • quoteVolume - Total value traded in 24h
  • count - Number of trades in 24h
  • bidPrice / askPrice - Best bid and ask
  • priceChangePercent - 24h price movement
GET /api/v3/depth?symbol=XXXYYY&limit=100 Order book snapshot (pending orders)
  • bids - Buy orders: [[price, quantity], ...]
  • asks - Sell orders: [[price, quantity], ...]
  • We sum orders within 0.1% of best price for "tight liquidity"

Understanding the Metrics

24h Volume Total value traded in the last 24 hours (actual executed trades)
24h Trades Number of individual trade executions in 24 hours
Spread Gap between best bid and ask. Lower = tighter market, better for trading
Bid Depth (0.1%) Total value of buy orders within 0.1% of best bid. How much you can sell without moving price
Ask Depth (0.1%) Total value of sell orders within 0.1% of best ask. How much you can buy without moving price
